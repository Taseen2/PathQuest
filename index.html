The Lost Lamp
of Varanasi

On the night of Deepawali, the whole city of Varanasi glows like a sky full of stars. Diyas shine on the ghats, drums echo in the streets, and the smell of sweets fills the air.

But tonight, something is wrong.

A sacred lamp called The Jyoti of Ganga has vanished from the old temple near the river. A priest whispers, "Without it, the festival may lose its blessing."

You are the only one close enough to find it.

A strange note lies on the temple steps:

"Find the lamp before the moon rises too high."

You take a deep breath and begin your journey.

Where do you go first?
